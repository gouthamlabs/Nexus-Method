# ⚠️ Phase 5 — Blind Spot & Counterfactual Analysis

> **Purpose:** Pre-mortem the analysis itself. Find what's missing, what assumptions could be wrong, and which perspective dominated.

---

## 📋 Prerequisites

- ✅ Master Directive loaded
- ✅ Phases 1-4 complete

---

## 🎯 Why This Phase Exists

Most AI research stops at "here are the findings." That's the moment maximum confidence meets maximum risk.

Phase 5 forces the AI to attack its own analysis using:

- **Pre-mortem** — "How could this be wrong?"
- **Counterfactual** — "What if a core assumption is false?"
- **Blind spot detection** — "What entire category did we miss?"
- **Bias audit** — "Which perspective dominated and skewed the synthesis?"

---

## 📜 The Prompt (Copy Below)

```
[NEXUS Phase 5 — Blind Spot & Counterfactual Analysis]

Attack the analysis you have produced so far. Apply the Master Directive —
brutal accuracy, no apologizing, no softening to protect prior conclusions.

1. PRE-MORTEM
   It is 2 years from now. The recommendations from this analysis were
   followed and they failed badly. What is the most likely failure mode?
   Trace the failure backward — which assumption broke first?

2. COUNTERFACTUAL ANALYSIS
   List the 3-5 most load-bearing assumptions in the analysis.
   For each, ask: what if this assumption is wrong?
   How much of the conclusion survives if it's wrong?

3. BLIND SPOT DETECTION
   What entire CATEGORIES of consideration are missing from the analysis?
   (Not just individual facts — full categories.)
   Examples: regulatory implications, second-order market effects,
   distributional impact, environmental cost, geopolitical exposure,
   technology dependencies, talent constraints, cultural factors.

4. BIAS AUDIT
   Which archetype's perspective dominated the synthesis?
   Was that domination earned (strongest evidence) or accidental
   (loudest voice / most fluent narrative)?
   Re-weight if necessary.

5. THE MISSING 10TH ARCHETYPE
   If you were to add ONE more archetype to NEXUS for this specific topic,
   what would it be and why?
   What would that archetype see that the standard 9 missed?

6. THE QUESTION I DIDN'T ASK
   What is the most important question I should have asked but didn't?
   How would the analysis change if I had asked it?

Output as a structured 6-section response.
Be aggressive. The goal is to find weaknesses BEFORE a real-world failure does.
```

---

## 📤 Expected Output

Six clearly-headed sections, with the pre-mortem and counterfactual treated as the most important. The "missing 10th archetype" and "the question I didn't ask" sections often produce the highest-value insights.

---

## 💡 Tips

- **Take the pre-mortem seriously.** If the AI traces a plausible failure path, that's a real risk — not a theoretical one.
- **Watch for category-level blind spots.** Individual facts can be checked; missing categories can't be retrofitted.
- **The "missing question" section is gold.** It often reveals that the original framing was structurally narrow.
