# 🔮 Phase 6 — Temporal Synthesis

> **Purpose:** Layer the analysis across past patterns, present reality, and future scenarios — with explicit Best / Base / Worst cases.

---

## 📋 Prerequisites

- ✅ Master Directive loaded
- ✅ Phases 1-5 complete

---

## 🎯 Why Temporal Layering Matters

Static analysis treats a problem as a snapshot. Real decisions play out over time, with:

- **Past patterns** that constrain what's possible (path dependence)
- **Present reality** that determines where we currently stand
- **Future scenarios** that diverge based on which decisions are made

NEXUS Phase 6 forces explicit treatment of all three.

---

## 📜 The Prompt (Copy Below)

```
[NEXUS Phase 6 — Temporal Synthesis]

Layer the analysis across three time horizons. Apply the Master Directive
throughout — no false certainty about the future, explicit confidence levels,
no hedging when patterns are clear.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PART A — PAST PATTERNS (Historian leads)

  1. What 3-5 historical situations are structurally similar to this one?
     Be specific: dates, names, outcomes.
  2. What pattern repeats? (Path dependence, regulatory ratchet,
     hype cycle, infrastructure transition, etc.)
  3. What did the historical situations get RIGHT?
  4. What did they get WRONG, and why?
  5. Confidence on the pattern match: HIGH / MODERATE / LOW / UNKNOWN

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PART B — PRESENT REALITY (Practitioner leads)

  1. What is actually happening on the ground RIGHT NOW?
     (Not the narrative — the operational reality.)
  2. What is the current consensus among practitioners?
  3. Where does the practitioner reality DIVERGE from the public narrative?
  4. What recent (last 12 months) developments materially changed the picture?
  5. Confidence on current state: HIGH / MODERATE / LOW / UNKNOWN

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PART C — FUTURE SCENARIOS (Futurist leads)

  Produce three explicit scenarios. For each, specify:

  🟢 BEST CASE (probability: X%)
     - What has to be true?
     - What does the world look like in 3-5 years if this plays out?
     - What's the leading indicator we'd see first?

  🟡 BASE CASE (probability: Y%)
     - The most likely outcome given current dynamics.
     - What does the world look like in 3-5 years?
     - What's the leading indicator?

  🔴 WORST CASE (probability: Z%)
     - The realistic downside (not catastrophic-tail, just bad).
     - What does the world look like in 3-5 years?
     - What's the leading indicator?

  Probabilities should sum to ~100% but mark them with confidence
  (e.g., "BASE CASE 60%, MODERATE confidence on probability").

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PART D — CROSS-TEMPORAL SYNTHESIS

  1. What does the historical pattern PREDICT about which scenario
     is most likely?
  2. What present-day signal would confirm we're moving toward
     which scenario?
  3. What is the EARLIEST observable signal that tells us which
     scenario is unfolding?
  4. If you had to commit to one decision today based on all three
     time horizons, what would it be?
```

---

## 📤 Expected Output

Four parts (A, B, C, D) with the three future scenarios clearly probability-weighted and tied to leading indicators. Part D produces the cross-temporal synthesis.

---

## 💡 Tips

- **Leading indicators are the most actionable output.** They tell you what to watch for to know which scenario is unfolding.
- **Be skeptical of high-confidence probabilities on future scenarios.** A confident-sounding 60% is usually 40-70% in reality.
- **The cross-temporal synthesis is what makes Phase 6 valuable.** Past + present + future together is harder than any single time horizon alone.
