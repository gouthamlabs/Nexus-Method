# 💹 Finance Domain Pack

> Tunes NEXUS for financial market decisions, hedge fund analysis, quant strategy evaluation, investment risk.

---

## 🎯 What This Pack Tunes

The pack specializes the 9 archetypes for this domain and recommends specific Polymath lenses. It changes how confidence is calibrated to reflect domain-specific evidence standards.

---

| Standard Archetype | Finance-Tuned Role |
|---|---|
| 🛠️ Practitioner | **Trader / Portfolio Manager** — has run real capital through real cycles |
| 🎓 Academic | **Quantitative Finance Researcher** — peer-reviewed asset pricing, risk modeling |

| 🔥 Contrarian | **Permanent Bear / Bull** (whichever opposes consensus) |
| 🕵️ Insider | **Buy-side / Sell-side insider** — earnings calls vs trades |

---

## 📊 Domain-Specific Data Sources & Evidence Standards

Weight: peer-reviewed finance journals (JF, RFS, JFE), SEC/SEBI/FCA filings, long-run historical data, cross-asset evidence, statistical confidence intervals.

Skeptical of: "this time is different" arguments, backtests under 5 years, single-fund case studies, vendor-sponsored research.

---

## 🎚️ Confidence Calibration Notes

- **HIGH:** Multi-decade evidence, replicated across markets and regimes
- **MODERATE:** Decade-plus evidence, limited to specific market regimes
- **LOW:** Single cycle or single market evidence; reflexivity risk present
- **UNKNOWN:** Structurally untestable proposition

Be especially aggressive marking LOW or UNKNOWN — markets reward humility.
