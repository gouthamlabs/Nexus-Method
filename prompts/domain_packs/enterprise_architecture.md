## 📊 Domain-Specific Data Sources & Evidence Standards

Weight: production case studies over vendor benchmarks, total cost of ownership over upfront licensing, post-mortems and incident reports, Conway's Law analysis of actual organizational structure, multi-year operational data.

Skeptical of: vendor-sponsored success stories, pilot metrics extrapolated to scale, "best practices" without specified context, reference architectures detached from organizational structure.

---

## 🎚️ Confidence Calibration Notes

- **HIGH:** Multiple production deployments at scale, across industries, converging post-mortems
- **MODERATE:** Validated at scale in a few cases; pilot-tested broadly
- **LOW:** Pilot-stage evidence, vendor claims, single-case studies
- **UNKNOWN:** Pre-pilot, theoretical, or vendor-only claims

Be aggressive about flagging LOW for pilot performance — pilot-to-scale gap is the dominant failure mode.
