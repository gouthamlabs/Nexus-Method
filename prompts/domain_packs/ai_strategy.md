## 📊 Domain-Specific Data Sources & Evidence Standards

Weight: production deployment results over benchmark scores, multi-model evaluations, independent evaluations, token economics math, regulatory trajectory as structural variable.

Skeptical of: capability claims based on cherry-picked examples, "X is solved" claims, benchmark performance without cost normalization, "scaling laws will solve it" without specifying conditions.

---

## 🎚️ Confidence Calibration Notes

- **HIGH:** Replicated across multiple labs, multiple model families, multi-quarter production validation
- **MODERATE:** Validated in production by 1-2 organizations
- **LOW:** Single-paper, single-demo, or vendor-only evidence
- **UNKNOWN:** Speculative scaling-extrapolation

**The half-life of HIGH confidence in AI is short. Re-grade every 90 days.**
