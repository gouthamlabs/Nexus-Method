# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in NEXUS Method a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or affiliation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Sharp intellectual disagreement without personal attack
- Brutal honesty about methodology weaknesses
- Crediting prior work (especially Stanford STORM and the mental models tradition)
- Calibrated confidence in claims

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Plagiarism or uncredited derivative work

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.
